How to Make A Boolish Peanut Butter and Jelly Sammy

Ingredients

  • Creamy delicious peanut butter
  • Sweet grape jelly
  • 2 slices of white bread, crusts cut off

Directions

  1. Lather and spread peanut butter over first slice of bread
  2. Carefully caress grape jelly throughout the second slice of bread
  3. Smack the two slices of bread together with boolishness
  4. Consume savory sammy

Approved by Peter Cembalest
