How to Make Awesome Toast

Ingredients

  • Creamy butter
  • Sweet grape jelly
  • 1 slice of white bread

Directions

  1. Pop that slice of bread into the toaster until it pops back up
  2. Carefully caress butter throughout the slice of bread as it melts
  3. Spread the grape jelly onto the bread with boolishness
  4. Consume lit toast
